About the blog

This blog is about immigration. Mostly I am writing about my experience of being an immigrant. But I also want to share stories of other immigrants and interesting insights about immigration. If you would like me to share your story on this blog, let me know.


Who am I?

I can describe myself in many ways. One of them is being an immigrant. I was born and raised in Poland. When I was 18, I went to Germany where I spent 5 years. After that I came to the UK - the place I call home now.
